About Us

Project Information

Welcome to the Organic Farming App! Our project is dedicated to providing a comprehensive platform for organic farming enthusiasts and practitioners. This application offers detailed step-by-step guides on growing various organic crops, including vegetables, fruits, grains, and flowers. Our goal is to support sustainable agriculture by offering practical insights and resources to help you successfully cultivate your organic garden.

Key features of the app include:

  • Step-by-step growth procedures for different crops.
  • Dynamic card display for various crop categories.
  • Interactive UI with modern design elements.
  • Live weather updates to help plan your farming activities.
  • Personalized recommendations based on user input.
  • Integration with local weather data for precise guidance.

About the Creator

This project was created by Group A3 , an engineering student passionate about sustainable agriculture and technology. With a background in software development and a keen interest in organic farming, Our Group aimed to develop a tool that bridges the gap between modern technology and traditional farming practices. The app is a result of Our Group dedication to promoting environmentally friendly farming methods and providing valuable resources to the farming community.
